About the Camp
Train With
The Best

Staffed by females currently playing at EAST!

Groups of 8 to 12 campers will work through the DEGS warm-up routine, develop fundamental technical skills alongside DEGS players, and compete in soccer-related games and small-sided matches.

All sessions are under the direction of Coach Jason Luzak — National A licensed and Downingtown East Girls Varsity head coach. This is a rare opportunity for young players to train with the real DEGS program.

What to Bring Each Day

  • Shin guards (required)
  • Water bottle
  • Soccer ball with your name on it
  • Appropriate footwear
  • Sunscreen & a positive attitude!
